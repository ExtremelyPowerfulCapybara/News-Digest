Satellite imagery confirms that Iran has damaged four of the eight early-warning radars underpinning the U.S. missile defense shield in the region, including the THAAD-linked AN/TPY-2 system and the AN/FPS-132 radar in Qatar, valued at nearly one billion dollars. España descarta bonificaciones directas en combustibles y opta por rebajas fiscales focalizadas en agricultores y transportistas
El ministro de Economía Carlos Cuerpo confirmó que el paquete anticrisis del Gobierno español no repetirá los descuentos universales de 20 céntimos por litro aplicados durante la guerra de Ucrania, señalando que todos los agentes sociales consultados prefieren medidas fiscales por su facilidad de implementación. Spain rules out blanket fuel subsidies, pivots to targeted tax cuts for farmers and truckers

Bitcoin surpasses 20 million units mined: over 95% of total supply that will ever exist is now in circulation
This week the 20 millionth bitcoin was mined, leaving fewer than one million units yet to be issued out of the 21 million programmed into its code — a milestone that Franklin Templeton and WisdomTree describe as a structural reinforcement of the scarcity narrative. España activa el reporte obligatorio de criptomonedas vía modelos 172, 175 y 721 en transposición de la directiva europea DAC8
El Ministerio de Hacienda español inició el proceso de audiencia pública para los nuevos modelos de declaración de activos digitales, exigiendo a exchanges y plataformas de custodia reportar saldos y transacciones detalladas de sus usuarios residentes, incluidas operaciones de staking, préstamos y airdrops. Spain activates mandatory crypto reporting via forms 172, 175, and 721 in transposition of EU's DAC8 directive
Spain's Ministry of Finance launched the public consultation process for new digital asset declaration models, requiring exchanges and custody platforms to report detailed balances and transactions of resident users, including staking, lending, and airdrop operations. The framework, which entered into force on January 1, 2026, transposes the EU's DAC8 Directive and aligns with OECD standards, though it excludes from automatic exchange holdings in self-custody wallets without a regulated intermediary. Spain's crypto sector is divided: some see a privacy erosion, others recognize that regulatory certainty is the price of institutional access.
